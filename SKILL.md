# Middle East Localization Skill

## Overview

This skill provides comprehensive localization support for Middle Eastern markets, with a focus on cultural adaptation, compliance, and market-specific content guidelines. It ensures products and services resonate authentically with local audiences while respecting cultural norms and regulatory requirements.

## Supported Markets

### Saudi Arabia (SA)

**Description:** Localization services for Saudi Arabia, including translation, cultural adaptation, and market research to ensure products and services resonate with the local audience.

#### Tone Guidelines
- **Allowed:** Formal, professional, neutral
- **Approach:** Maintain respect and professionalism in all communications
- **Example:** Use "We welcome your inquiry" rather than casual language

#### Terminology Mapping
- **Forbidden Terms:** Wine, alcohol
- **Mappings:**
  - Wine glasses → Juice glasses
  - Wine goblet → Goblet
- **Rationale:** Align with Islamic cultural norms and local sensitivities

#### Behavior Standards
- **Allowed:** Respectful, culturally sensitive, enforce brand safety guidelines
- **Forbidden:** Offensive language, stereotypes
- **Guidelines:**
  - Treat cultural differences with respect
  - Avoid gender stereotypes or culturally inappropriate content
  - Ensure all imagery and messaging aligns with brand values

#### Compliance Requirements
- Adhere to local regulations and cultural norms
- Ensure all content is appropriate for the Saudi Arabian market
- Reference local laws and guidelines in content decisions
- Obtain necessary approvals for culturally sensitive content

## Usage

### When to Use This Skill

- **Content Translation:** Translating marketing copy, UI strings, documentation for Saudi Arabia
- **Cultural Adaptation:** Modifying content tone, terminology, imagery for regional appropriateness
- **Compliance Review:** Validating content against local regulations and cultural guidelines
- **Market Research:** Understanding local preferences and market dynamics
- **Brand Safety:** Ensuring messaging aligns with brand guidelines and avoids cultural offense

### How to Use This Skill

1. **Identify Your Content:** Provide the content that needs localization
2. **Specify the Market:** Indicate the target market (e.g., Saudi Arabia)
3. **Apply Guidelines:** The skill will apply tone, terminology, and behavior rules
4. **Review for Compliance:** Validate against local regulations and cultural norms
5. **Adapt and Finalize:** Make adjustments and finalize localized content

## Key Principles

1. **Cultural Respect:** Always prioritize cultural sensitivity and respect
2. **Compliance First:** Ensure adherence to local laws and regulations
3. **Authenticity:** Localize rather than merely translate
4. **Brand Consistency:** Maintain brand identity while adapting to local context
5. **Quality Assurance:** Review all localized content for accuracy and appropriateness
